# Best SQL Questions

Use this file to keep only strong interview-style questions, not every random practice question.

## Core Questions

1. Find the second highest salary.
2. Find duplicate rows in a table.
3. Delete duplicate rows while keeping one copy.
4. Find employees earning more than their department average.
5. Find the top `N` salaries in each department.
6. Find the nth highest salary using window functions.
7. Find customers who never placed an order.
8. Find customers who placed orders in consecutive days.
9. Find monthly revenue and month-over-month growth.
10. Find products with highest sales in each category.
11. Find running total of sales by date.
12. Find moving average of sales over 7 days.
13. Rank records with `RANK`, `DENSE_RANK`, and `ROW_NUMBER`.
14. Pivot category totals into columns.
15. Find retention or repeat customers from event data.

## Dataset Mapping Ideas

- Employee dataset:
  salaries, departments, managers, join dates
- Orders dataset:
  customers, orders, order_items, products, payments
- Sales dataset:
  dates, stores, categories, quantity, revenue
- Movie dataset:
  movies, ratings, genres, users
- Retail dataset:
  invoices, stock, products, regions
